main charater

In an island paradise populated entirely by happy, flightless birds or almost entirely, Red, a bird with a temper problem, speedy Chuck, and the volatile Bomb have always been outsiders. But when the island is visited by mysterious green piggies, it’s up to these unlikely outcasts to figure out what the pigs are up to.

  • Red
    Red is an angry bird who lives on Bird Island, a world populated with the happiest birds imaginable. Needless to say, Red doesn’t fit in. He has few friends, can’t hold down a job thanks to his temper and lives alone on the outskirts of town. What he really wants, but doesn’t know yet, is for the birds to accept him.
  • Chuck
    Everything about Chuck is speedy. He thinks fast, talks fast and moves even faster. He zips around the island in a flash and jumps from one thought to another.
  • Bomb
    Bomb is a bit dim but has a big heart. He’s the only bird on the island who actually explodes when surprised or stressed. He’s determined to gain control of his powers, but still has a long way to go!

Terence is a mystery. He’s the largest of all the birds and uses his muscle when needed. He talks only in growls and groans. Everyone is a little afraid of Terence but deep down, he is actually a gentle giant.

Stella is the expert on Bird Island. She is proud of her home and makes it her business to learn pretty much everything going on in the bird village. She is also the tour guide for the island.
